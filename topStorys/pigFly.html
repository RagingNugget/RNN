RNN News Presenting - Flying Pigs

PIGS

Genetically modified organisms allowed for scientists to create pigs with the ability to fly. After years of research, at 76 years of age, scientist Dr. Leonardo Flore and his team have created the first piglet with wings and the ability to fly.

“Take that flightless birds” Flore comments on his development.

At the age of 19, Flore’s comrade, Doug Miller sold his sister’s hand in marriage in a bluff “when pigs fly”. Since that fateful day, creating flying pigs has been Flore’s quest to win over his alleged ideal woman.

“My heart has belonged to that woman since I laid eyes on her. When Doug discovered it was I, her secret admirer; we had a bit of a falling out. His last words to me, ‘you can marry my sister when pigs fly.’ thus this lifelong dedication” Flore flashbacks to recall the day.

However with this new discovery, Flore has disrupted the flow of society. His inbox flooded with complaints of lives ruined, people so far in debt, and abolished relationships.

“Just last week I told my daughter she could have a pet unicorn when pigs fly. She’s five. How in the world am I supposed to tell her they don’t exist?” One of the letters reads.

Flore continues on with no remorse for the dwindling society, now that the girl is in his grasp. The two plan on getting married and living the rest of their days as husband and wife with their piglet Alfonso.

With this new chaos descended, who knows what the future holds, robots may even start the next apocalypse. Only time will tell.
